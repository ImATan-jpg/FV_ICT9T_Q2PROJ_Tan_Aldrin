Lechon Kawali (Pan-Roasted Pork Belly)

$14.99

Serving Size:

Sauce Options:

Traditional Lechon Kawali Recipe

Ingredients:

    For the Pork:

  • 2 lbs pork belly, whole piece
  • 2 tablespoons salt
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tablespoon whole peppercorns
  • 6 cloves garlic, crushed
  • Water for boiling
  • Oil for deep frying

    • For the Dipping Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Ground black pepper
  • Chili peppers (optional)

Instructions:

    Initial Preparation:

  1. Boil pork with salt, bay leaves, peppercorns, and garlic
  2. Simmer until tender (about 1 hour)
  3. Remove from water and let dry completely
  4. Refrigerate overnight to dry further

    5. Frying:

  5. Heat oil in a deep pan
  6. Deep fry the pork until golden and crispy
  7. Drain on paper towels

    8. Sauce Preparation:

  8. Mix vinegar, garlic, pepper, and chili
  9. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving

Chef's Tips:

  • Score the skin before boiling for better crackling
  • Ensure pork is completely dry before frying
  • Maintain oil temperature for even cooking
  • Cut into serving pieces only after frying
