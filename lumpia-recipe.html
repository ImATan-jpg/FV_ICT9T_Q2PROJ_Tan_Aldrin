Traditional Lumpiang Shanghai Recipe

Ingredients:

    For the Filling:

  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 1/2 lb ground chicken
  • 1 cup carrots, minced
  • 1 cup onions, minced
  • 1/2 cup celery, minced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • Other Items:

  • 50 pieces lumpia wrapper
  • 1 egg (for sealing)
  • Oil for frying

    • Sweet Chili Sauce:

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons chili flakes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Instructions:

    Filling Preparation:

  1. Mix all filling ingredients thoroughly
  2. Season with salt and pepper

    3. Rolling:

  3. Place 2 tablespoons filling on wrapper
  4. Roll tightly, sealing with egg wash
  5. Cut rolls in half if desired

    6. Cooking:

  6. Heat oil in deep pan
  7. Fry until golden brown
  8. Drain on paper towels

    9. Sauce Making:

  9. Combine sauce ingredients in pan
  10. Simmer until thickened
  11. Cool before serving

Chef's Tips:

  • Keep wrappers covered while working
  • Don't overfill the lumpia
  • Maintain oil temperature at 350°F
  • Can be frozen for later use
