Arroz Caldo (Filipino Chicken Porridge)

$7.99

Serving Size:

Toppings:

Traditional Filipino Arroz Caldo Recipe

Ingredients:

    For the Porridge:

  • 2 lbs chicken pieces
  • 2 cups glutinous rice (malagkit)
  • 2 tablespoons minced ginger
  • 1 whole garlic bulb, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce (patis)
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 8-10 cups chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • For Toppings:

  • Fried minced garlic
  • Chopped green onions
  • Calamansi or lemon wedges
  • Hard-boiled eggs
  • Additional fish sauce

Instructions:

    Preparation:

  1. Sauté garlic, ginger, and onion until fragrant
  2. Add chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned
  3. Add rice and stir to coat with oil
  4. Pour in chicken broth and fish sauce
  5. Simmer until rice is very soft (30-40 minutes)

    6. Finishing:

  6. Shred chicken if desired
  7. Adjust consistency with more broth if needed
  8. Season with salt and pepper
  9. Top with garnishes before serving

Chef's Tips:

  • Use bone-in chicken for better flavor
  • Make extra fried garlic for topping
  • Perfect for rainy days or when feeling under the weather
