Ginataang Baboy (Pork in Coconut Milk)

$12.99

Serving Size:

Spice Level:

Traditional Ginataang Baboy Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs pork belly, cubed
  • 2 cans coconut milk
  • 1 can coconut cream
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 thumb-sized ginger, julienned
  • 4 pieces green chilies
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • Ground black pepper
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

    Initial Preparation:

  1. Sauté garlic, ginger, and onions
  2. Add pork and brown slightly
  3. Season with fish sauce

    4. Cooking:

  4. Pour in coconut milk
  5. Simmer until pork is tender
  6. Add coconut cream
  7. Add green chilies

    8. Finishing:

  8. Reduce sauce to desired thickness
  9. Adjust seasoning
  10. Serve hot with rice

Chef's Tips:

  • Use fresh coconut milk if possible
  • Don't stir too often to prevent curdling
  • Cook until oil separates from cream
  • Adjust chili amount to taste
