Pancit Canton (Filipino Stir-Fried Noodles)

$11.99

Serving Size:

Protein Choice:

Traditional Pancit Canton Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pancit canton noodles
  • 1/2 lb chicken, sliced
  • 1/4 lb pork, sliced
  • 1 cup carrots, julienned
  • 1 cup cabbage, chopped
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 cup celery, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions for garnish

Instructions:

    Preparation:

  1. Sauté garlic and onions
  2. Add chicken and pork, cook until brown
  3. Add vegetables except cabbage
  4. Pour in soy sauce and oyster sauce

    5. Noodles:

  5. Add chicken broth
  6. Add noodles and mix well
  7. Add cabbage last
  8. Season with salt and pepper

    9. Finishing:

  9. Garnish with green onions
  10. Serve with calamansi

Chef's Tips:

  • Don't overcook the noodles
  • Add vegetables in order of cooking time
  • Adjust sauce to taste
  • Best served fresh and hot
