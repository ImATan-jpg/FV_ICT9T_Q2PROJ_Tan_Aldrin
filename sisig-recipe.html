Sizzling Sisig

$13.99

Serving Size:

Add-ons:

Traditional Sizzling Sisig Recipe

Ingredients:

    For the Meat:

  • 1 lb pork face/ears
  • 1/2 lb pork belly
  • 1/2 lb chicken liver

    • For Seasoning:

  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 onions, finely chopped
  • 5 Thai chilies, chopped
  • 1/4 cup calamansi juice
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Ground black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • Mayonnaise (optional)

    • For Serving:

  • Raw egg
  • Calamansi wedges
  • Hot sizzling plate

Instructions:

    Meat Preparation:

  1. Boil pork until tender
  2. Grill until slightly charred
  3. Chop into small pieces

    4. Assembly:

  4. Sauté garlic and onions
  5. Add chopped meat and liver
  6. Season with soy sauce and calamansi
  7. Add chilies and pepper

    8. Serving:

  8. Transfer to hot sizzling plate
  9. Top with raw egg
  10. Serve immediately with calamansi

Chef's Tips:

  • Chop meat while still warm
  • Keep meat pieces uniform in size
  • Serve on a very hot plate
  • Mix egg immediately when served
