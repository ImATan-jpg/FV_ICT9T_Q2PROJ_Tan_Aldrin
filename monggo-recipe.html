Monggo Guisado (Sautéed Mung Beans)

$9.99

Serving Size:

Add-ons:

Traditional Monggo Guisado Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mung beans
  • 1/2 lb pork belly, diced
  • 2 bunches spinach/malunggay
  • 1 cup chicharon
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • Ground black pepper
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

    Bean Preparation:

  1. Soak mung beans for 1-2 hours
  2. Boil until tender (30-40 minutes)
  3. Mash half of the beans

    4. Cooking:

  4. Sauté garlic, onions, and tomatoes
  5. Add pork and cook until brown
  6. Add cooked mung beans
  7. Season with fish sauce and pepper

    8. Finishing:

  8. Add spinach/malunggay
  9. Top with chicharon
  10. Adjust seasoning to taste

Chef's Tips:

  • Soak beans for faster cooking
  • Add chicharon just before serving
  • Don't overcook the greens
  • Best served with rice
