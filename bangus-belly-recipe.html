Daing na Bangus Belly (Marinated Milkfish Belly)

$11.99

Serving Size:

Side Options:

Traditional Bangus Belly Recipe

Ingredients:

    For the Fish:

  • 4 pieces bangus belly
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 8 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tablespoon peppercorns
  • Salt to taste

    • For Serving:

  • Cooking oil
  • Tomatoes
  • Red onions
  • Spiced vinegar

Instructions:

    Marination:

  1. Clean bangus belly thoroughly
  2. Mix vinegar, garlic, pepper, and salt
  3. Marinate fish for 30 minutes

    4. Cooking:

  4. Heat oil in a pan
  5. Fry bangus until golden brown
  6. Drain excess oil

    7. Serving:

  7. Prepare tomato-onion side salad
  8. Serve with garlic rice
  9. Add spiced vinegar on the side

Chef's Tips:

  • Don't over-marinate to avoid too much acidity
  • Pat dry before frying
  • Maintain medium heat while frying
  • Best served for breakfast
