Sotanghon Soup (Glass Noodle Soup)

Traditional Sotanghon Soup Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz sotanghon (glass noodles)
  • 1/2 lb chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups cabbage, chopped
  • 2 carrots, julienned
  • 1 cup celery, chopped
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • Green onions for garnish
  • Ground black pepper
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

    Preparation:

  1. Soak glass noodles in warm water
  2. Prepare all vegetables
  3. Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces

    4. Cooking:

  4. Sauté garlic and onions
  5. Add chicken and cook until brown
  6. Pour in chicken broth
  7. Add carrots and celery
  8. Add soaked noodles
  9. Add cabbage last

    10. Finishing:

  10. Season with fish sauce and pepper
  11. Garnish with green onions
  12. Serve hot

Chef's Tips:

  • Don't oversoak the noodles
  • Add vegetables in order of cooking time
  • Adjust broth consistency as needed
  • Perfect for rainy days
