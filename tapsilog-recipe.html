Traditional Filipino Tapsilog Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb beef sirloin, sliced thinly
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons calamansi juice or lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 cups cooked garlic rice
  • 4 fried eggs
  • Cooking oil

    • For the Garlic Rice:

  • 4 cups cooked white rice
  • 6-8 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

    For the Tapa (Beef):

  1. Combine soy sauce, calamansi juice, garlic, and pepper in a bowl
  2. Add beef slices and marinate for at least 3 hours or overnight
  3. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat
  4. Cook marinated beef until browned and slightly crispy

    5. For the Garlic Rice:

  5. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat
  6. Sauté minced garlic until golden brown
  7. Add cooked rice and stir-fry until well combined
  8. Season with salt to taste

    9. Assembly:

  9. Fry eggs sunny side up
  10. Serve tapa with garlic rice and fried egg
  11. Optional: Serve with vinegar dipping sauce and sliced tomatoes
