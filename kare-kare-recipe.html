Kare-Kare (Filipino Oxtail Peanut Stew)

$15.99

Serving Size:

Add-ons:

Traditional Kare-Kare Recipe

Ingredients:

    For the Meat:

  • 2 lbs oxtail, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 lb beef tripe (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • For the Sauce:

  • 1 cup ground peanuts
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup rice flour
  • 3 tablespoons annatto powder
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced

    • Vegetables:

  • 1 bundle string beans
  • 2 eggplants, sliced
  • 1 bundle banana heart, sliced
  • Bok choy

    • To Serve:

  • Bagoong alamang (shrimp paste)
  • Steamed rice

Instructions:

    Meat Preparation:

  1. Boil oxtail and tripe until tender (2-3 hours)
  2. Reserve the broth

    3. Sauce Making:

  3. Sauté garlic and onions
  4. Add ground peanuts and peanut butter
  5. Add rice flour dissolved in water
  6. Add annatto powder
  7. Pour in reserved broth

    8. Assembly:

  8. Add cooked meat to sauce
  9. Cook vegetables separately
  10. Arrange vegetables on top
  11. Serve with bagoong alamang

Chef's Tips:

  • Cook meat until very tender
  • Don't overcook vegetables
  • Adjust sauce thickness as needed
  • Bagoong is essential for authentic taste
