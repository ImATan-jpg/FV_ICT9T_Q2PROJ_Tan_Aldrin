Chicken Adobo

$12.99

Serving Size:

Rice Option:

Traditional Filipino Adobo Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken pieces
  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 whole garlic head, crushed
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tablespoon whole peppercorns
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt to taste
  • Cooking oil

Instructions:

  1. Combine chicken, vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns in a pot
  2. Marinate for 30 minutes
  3. Add water and bring to a boil
  4. Reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes or until chicken is tender
  5. Remove chicken and set aside
  6. Reduce sauce until thickened
  7. Heat oil in a pan and fry chicken until brown
  8. Pour reduced sauce over chicken
  9. Serve hot with steamed rice
