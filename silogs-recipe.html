Silogs (Filipino Breakfast Plates)

Choose Your Silog:

Egg Style:

Basic Silog Components

Essential Components:

    Garlic Rice (Sinangag):

  • 4 cups cooked rice
  • 8-10 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt to taste
  • Cooking oil

    • Fried Egg (Itlog):

  • Fresh eggs
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cooking oil

    • Common Sides:

  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Atchara (pickled papaya)
  • Vinegar dipping sauce

Instructions:

    Garlic Rice:

  1. Sauté minced garlic until golden
  2. Add day-old rice
  3. Season with salt and stir-fry

    4. Perfect Fried Egg:

  4. Heat oil in a pan
  5. Crack egg and cook to preference
  6. Season with salt and pepper

    7. Assembly:

  7. Place garlic rice on plate
  8. Add chosen protein (tapa, tocino, or longganisa)
  9. Top with fried egg
  10. Serve with sides

Chef's Tips:

  • Use day-old rice for better garlic rice
  • Don't overcrowd the pan when frying eggs
  • Prepare vinegar dipping sauce with chilies
