Traditional Crispy Pata Recipe

Ingredients:

    For Boiling:

  • 1 whole pork leg, cleaned
  • 2 tablespoons salt
  • 2 tablespoons peppercorns
  • 6 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • Water for boiling

    • For Deep Frying:

  • Oil for deep frying
  • Rock salt

    • For Dipping Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • Chopped garlic
  • Chili peppers
  • Ground black pepper

Instructions:

    Initial Preparation:

  1. Clean pork leg thoroughly
  2. Boil with spices until tender (1-2 hours)
  3. Drain and air dry overnight in refrigerator

    4. Deep Frying:

  4. Heat oil in a deep pan
  5. Deep fry pata until golden and crispy
  6. Drain excess oil

    7. Sauce Preparation:

  7. Mix vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and peppers
  8. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving

Chef's Tips:

  • Ensure pork is completely dry before frying
  • Maintain oil temperature at 350-375°F
  • Use a splatter guard when frying
  • Let rest for 5 minutes before chopping
