Tocilog (Tocino with Rice and Egg)

$9.49

Serving Size:

Rice Option:

Traditional Filipino Tocilog Recipe

Ingredients:

    For the Tocino:

  • 1 lb pork belly, sliced thinly
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons salt
  • 2 tablespoons garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon anise wine (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon red food coloring (optional)

    • For Serving:

  • 4 cups cooked garlic rice
  • 4 fried eggs
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Vinegar dipping sauce

Instructions:

    Preparing the Tocino:

  1. Mix sugar, salt, garlic powder, and food coloring
  2. Coat pork slices with mixture
  3. Marinate for 24-48 hours
  4. Cook in a pan with a little oil and water
  5. Fry until caramelized and slightly crispy

    6. Assembly:

  6. Prepare garlic rice
  7. Fry eggs sunny side up
  8. Serve tocino with rice and egg
  9. Garnish with tomatoes

Chef's Tips:

  • Add water while cooking tocino for tender meat
  • Let it caramelize at the end for best flavor
  • Serve with spiced vinegar
