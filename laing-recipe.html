Laing (Dried Taro Leaves in Coconut Milk)

$10.99

Serving Size:

Spice Level:

Traditional Bicolano Laing Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups dried taro leaves
  • 2 cans coconut milk
  • 1 can coconut cream
  • 1/2 lb pork belly, sliced
  • 10 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 thumb-sized ginger, minced
  • 3 onions, chopped
  • 10 Thai chili peppers
  • 2 pieces shrimp paste
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

    Initial Preparation:

  1. Sauté garlic, ginger, and onions
  2. Add pork and cook until brown
  3. Add shrimp paste

    4. Cooking Process:

  4. Pour in coconut milk
  5. Add dried taro leaves (don't stir)
  6. Add chili peppers
  7. Simmer until liquid reduces

    8. Finishing:

  8. Add coconut cream
  9. Season with salt and pepper
  10. Simmer until oil surfaces

Chef's Tips:

  • Don't stir until leaves are fully cooked
  • Adjust spiciness to preference
  • Cook until oil separates from cream
  • Best served with steamed rice
