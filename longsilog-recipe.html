Traditional Filipino Longsilog Recipe

Ingredients:

    For the Longganisa:

  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 1/4 cup minced garlic
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika (optional)

    • For Serving:

  • 4 cups cooked garlic rice
  • 4 fried eggs
  • Cooking oil
  • Vinegar dipping sauce
  • Sliced tomatoes (optional)

Instructions:

    For the Longganisa:

  1. Mix ground pork with garlic, soy sauce, vinegar, brown sugar, salt, pepper, and paprika
  2. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour or overnight for better flavor
  3. Shape mixture into small sausages (about 2-3 inches long)
  4. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat
  5. Cook longganisa for 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally until browned and fully cooked

    6. For the Garlic Rice:

  6. Heat oil in a pan and sauté minced garlic until golden
  7. Add cooked rice and stir-fry until well combined
  8. Season with salt to taste

    9. Assembly:

  9. Fry eggs sunny side up
  10. Serve longganisa with garlic rice and fried egg
  11. Garnish with sliced tomatoes
  12. Serve with vinegar dipping sauce on the side

Chef's Tips:

  • For juicier longganisa, add a small amount of water while cooking and let it evaporate
  • You can adjust the sweetness and garlic amount to your preference
  • Traditional longganisa is usually served with spiced vinegar
