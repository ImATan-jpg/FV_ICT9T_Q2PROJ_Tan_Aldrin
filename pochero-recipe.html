Pochero (Filipino Beef Stew)

$14.99

Serving Size:

Meat Choice:

Traditional Pochero Recipe

Ingredients:

    Main Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs beef chuck, cubed
  • 2 pieces chorizo de bilbao
  • 2 large potatoes, quartered
  • 2 sweet potatoes, quartered
  • 1 small cabbage, wedged
  • 2 plantains, sliced
  • 1 can garbanzo beans
  • 1 bundle string beans
  • 2 corn cobs, cut
  • 1 bunch pechay

    • Sauce Base:

  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 tablespoon peppercorns
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Fish sauce to taste

Instructions:

    Initial Preparation:

  1. Boil beef until tender (1-2 hours)
  2. Sauté garlic, onions, and tomatoes
  3. Add chorizo and beef

    4. Adding Vegetables:

  4. Add potatoes and sweet potatoes
  5. Add corn and plantains
  6. Add garbanzo beans
  7. Add remaining vegetables

    8. Finishing:

  8. Season with fish sauce
  9. Add pechay last
  10. Simmer until vegetables are cooked

Chef's Tips:

  • Cook beef until very tender
  • Add vegetables in order of cooking time
  • Don't overcook the vegetables
  • Serve hot with rice
