Champorado (Chocolate Rice Porridge)

$6.99

Serving Size:

Add-ons:

Traditional Filipino Champorado Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups glutinous rice
  • 8 cups water
  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate, chopped
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar (adjust to taste)
  • Pinch of salt

    • For Serving:

  • Condensed milk
  • Fresh milk
  • Dried fish (tuyo) - optional

Instructions:

    Cooking Process:

  1. Bring water to a boil in a pot
  2. Add glutinous rice and stir occasionally
  3. When rice is half-cooked, add cocoa powder
  4. Add chopped chocolate and sugar
  5. Simmer until rice is fully cooked and porridge is thick

    6. Serving:

  6. Pour into bowls while hot
  7. Add condensed milk or fresh milk
  8. Serve with dried fish if desired

Chef's Tips:

  • Use tablea (pure cocoa tablets) for authentic flavor
  • Adjust liquid consistency to preference
  • Best served hot for breakfast
