Lugaw (Filipino Rice Porridge)

$6.49

Serving Size:

Toppings:

Traditional Filipino Lugaw Recipe

Ingredients:

    For the Porridge:

  • 1 cup rice (malagkit or regular)
  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons minced ginger
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • For Toppings:

  • Fried garlic
  • Chopped green onions
  • Hard-boiled eggs
  • Calamansi
  • Chicharon (pork cracklings)

Instructions:

    Basic Preparation:

  1. Sauté garlic, ginger, and onion
  2. Add rice and toast slightly
  3. Pour in chicken broth
  4. Simmer until rice is very soft (25-30 minutes)
  5. Stir occasionally and add more broth if needed

    6. Serving:

  6. Season with salt and pepper
  7. Top with fried garlic and green onions
  8. Add desired toppings
  9. Serve hot with calamansi

Chef's Tips:

  • Use glutinous rice for thicker consistency
  • Make extra fried garlic for topping
  • Adjust liquid based on preferred thickness
