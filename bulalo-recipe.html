Bulalo (Beef Marrow Soup)

$16.99

Serving Size:

Add-ons:

Traditional Bulalo Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs beef shank with marrow
  • 2 corn cobs, quartered
  • 1 bundle pechay
  • 2 potatoes, quartered
  • 2 onions, quartered
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 tablespoons peppercorns
  • Salt to taste
  • Fish sauce
  • Green onions for garnish

Instructions:

    Initial Cooking:

  1. Boil beef shanks until scum rises
  2. Drain and clean the meat
  3. Boil in fresh water with garlic and onions
  4. Simmer for 2-3 hours until tender

    5. Adding Vegetables:

  5. Add corn and simmer for 10 minutes
  6. Add potatoes
  7. Add pechay last

    8. Finishing:

  8. Season with salt and pepper
  9. Garnish with green onions
  10. Serve hot with fish sauce

Chef's Tips:

  • Choose shanks with large marrow bones
  • Simmer on low heat for clear broth
  • Don't overcook the vegetables
  • Serve with patis and calamansi
