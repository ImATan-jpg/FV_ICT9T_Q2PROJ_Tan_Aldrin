Bangusilog (Milkfish with Rice and Egg)

$10.99

Serving Size:

Rice Option:

Traditional Filipino Bangusilog Recipe

Ingredients:

    For the Bangus (Milkfish):

  • 1 whole bangus (milkfish), cleaned and butterflied
  • 4 tablespoons vinegar
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Cooking oil for frying

    • For Serving:

  • 4 cups cooked garlic rice
  • 4 fried eggs
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Vinegar-soy dipping sauce

Instructions:

    Preparing the Bangus:

  1. Clean and butterfly the bangus, removing all bones if desired
  2. Mix vinegar, garlic, pepper, and salt
  3. Marinate the fish for 30 minutes
  4. Heat oil in a large pan
  5. Fry the bangus until golden brown and crispy

    6. Assembly:

  6. Prepare garlic rice
  7. Fry eggs sunny side up
  8. Serve bangus with rice and egg
  9. Garnish with tomatoes
  10. Serve with vinegar-soy dipping sauce

Chef's Tips:

  • Score the fish before marinating for better flavor absorption
  • Don't overcrowd the pan when frying
  • Pat the fish dry before frying for crispier skin
