Classic Latte Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 shots of espresso
  • 6-8 oz steamed milk
  • 1-2 cm milk foam

Instructions:

  1. Pull two shots of espresso into a latte cup
  2. Steam milk to 150-155°F
  3. Pour steamed milk over espresso
  4. Top with a small layer of milk foam
  5. Optional: Create latte art design
