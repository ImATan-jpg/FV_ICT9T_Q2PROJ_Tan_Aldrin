Sinigang na Baboy (Pork Sour Soup)

$13.99

Serving Size:

Spice Level:

Traditional Sinigang na Baboy Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs pork belly, cut into chunks
  • 2 packs sinigang mix
  • 2 tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 2 eggplants, sliced
  • 2 cups string beans
  • 1 bunch water spinach (kangkong)
  • 4 pieces green chili
  • 2 pieces radish, sliced
  • 8 cups water
  • Fish sauce to taste
  • Ground black pepper

Instructions:

    Preparation:

  1. Boil water in a large pot
  2. Add pork and simmer until tender (30-40 mins)
  3. Add onions and tomatoes
  4. Add sinigang mix

    5. Adding Vegetables:

  5. Add radish and eggplant
  6. Add string beans
  7. Add green chilies
  8. Finally, add kangkong

    9. Finishing:

  9. Season with fish sauce
  10. Add pepper to taste
  11. Simmer until vegetables are cooked

Chef's Tips:

  • Use pork with bones for better flavor
  • Add vegetables in order of cooking time
  • Serve hot with rice
  • Can substitute tamarind powder for sinigang mix
